About Us

Inspired by lockdown state sanctioned daily walks, which turned into long meandering strolls centered around a picnic with (socially distanced) friends, Picnic Hamper Bolton wants to take the fiddly bit out of your day out.

We currently have two delicious bespoke menu options, with something to suit every taste. Our emphasis is on keeping the great outdoors as green as possible, so our treats (and every single one of their ingredients) is picked up as locally as possible, then carefully wrapped in minimal packaging, and presented in an easily transported but most importantly returnable picnic hamper backpack which we then clean and reuse.
