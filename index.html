Welcome to Picnic Hamper Bolton

An independent, eco-friendly, locally sourced caterer on the edge of the West Pennine Moors.

Here at Picnic Hamper Bolton, we feel strongly about the quality of your picnics. This is why we only used as local as possible food sources, which will ensure fresh ingredients and hitting those high catering standards you would expect.

Our menus are specifically designed to suit your day out. By having a delightful selection of our favourite picks in each menu, it takes out the clutter of wrapping and containers that are not kind to our world!

If you would like to browse our menus, please hit the menu tab! If you would prefer to get to know us further, please check out our about us page!
